TURN ON THE LIGHT

Animation promoting ecology

Personal project

Cooperation: Natalia Trojanowska

July 2020

Animation tells a story of Lamp that neglects simple rules of eco–life. The Lamp is quickly cautioned by others and it realises what a damage it has caused to it’s surrounding. I was working in a group of two. Movie was made with cut–out method. Characters were drew with markers, scaned and animated using Adobe After Effects. We received finalists status at Animafantasia contest.
