ROMANTYCZNA A PRZECIEŻ ROZWAŻNA

Design of the book

Personal project

Author: Małgorzatta Lortsch—Krawczenko

Editing: Maria Kotowska—Kachel

Warsaw 2021

The book was developed and published in 2020/2021. It is a story about the author’s grandmother with memories from her diary from the turn of the 19th and 20th centuries. It consists of the base text, excerpts from the diary, the author’s e-mail conversations with the editor, and photos. The entire graphic design was created in cooperation with the author.
