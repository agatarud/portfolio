INSCRIPTIONS EN RELATION

Edited by Ruedi Baur, Civic City

Design: Ruedi Baur, Laura Martínez, Odyssée Khorsandian, Maxime Leleux, Agata Rudnicka

During 5 months internship in Paris I started working on the book that was a sum up of the Inscriptions en Relation project. Book was divided into two parts-one on the left side was leaded upside down and presented projects from different schools from all around the world and the other on the right side gathered research articles written by well known graphic designers, as well as interviews and transcriptions from a conference.
