NEW YORK TIMES COVER

Mock Sunday Review cover illustration

Tutor: Alexandra Zsigmond

April 2020

Cover was made during workshops with NYT Art Director Alexandra Zsigmond. It’s refering to an article on the people’s behaviour during coronavirus pandemic and resemblance to previous reactions to pandemics in human history. Illustration is reffering to the fact that during every epidemic politicians underestimate the problem and put a gloss on it.
