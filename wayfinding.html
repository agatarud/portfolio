WAYFINDING SYSTEM FOR PJAIT

Wayfinding system for building A of Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology

Cooperation: Madina Mahomedova, Illia Dolbniev, Maciej Wawer

Tutors: Aleksandra Krupa, Witański Bartłomiej

January 2021

In this project I was working in a group of four. We decided to combine traditional, japanese patterns with modern colors and shapes. Icons were designed in such a way to resemble Japanese katakana and hiragana characters. I was working mostly on creating patterns and leading lines inspired by modern, japanese posters and metro identification.
